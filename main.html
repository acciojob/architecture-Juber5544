Architecture: The Art of Building

Architecture is the art and science of designing and constructing buildings. It plays a vital role in shaping the environment we live in, combining aesthetics, functionality, and structural integrity.

Architecture reflects culture, history, and technological advancements. From ancient wonders to modern skyscrapers, architecture defines our cities and societies.
Sustainability is a growing focus in architecture, with eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient designs becoming a priority in modern construction.
